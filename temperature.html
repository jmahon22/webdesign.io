Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This scatter plot compares the latitudes and max temperatures of cities in our dataset. Temperatures closest to the equator (0) appear to be highest overall. Temperatures drop slightly for locations in the southern hemisphere (negative latitudes), but we see a more significant drop in temperature the more north of the equator we go (positive latitudes)
